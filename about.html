Why and How?

This app's objective is to help you manage and track your goals during your life.

  • Are you thinking of losing weight or starting running?
  • Do you want to stop smoking?
  • Do you really know how much money do you spend paying your bills?
  • Maybe you are trying to create metrics about some event of your life?
  • Do you want to gather real useful information about your daily habits, so you can desing a better life?

If you answered "yes" to any of these questions this app is for you.

Lifestyle tracker will help you to measure your performance and gather some statistics about it!!!

Start tracking is really easy. We will resume it in three simple steps:

  1. Add a tracker. In order to do that tap on the "add tracker" button, provide a name and the kind of measure you are going to take (number and yes/no values (more in the future))
  2. Tap on the "add data" button, choose your tracker, add a value and a date. (you can add data inside a concrete track too).
  3. Tap on your tracker. Inside it, you will be able to find a graphic showing your progress, some statistics and all the entered data related with this tracker!

Of course you will also find the typical options as remove/edit trackers and values.

And that's all!
